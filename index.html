Game Rules

Simple rules that make Padel accessible to everyone while maintaining competitive excitement

Basic Rules

1

Scoring System

Same as tennis: 15, 30, 40, game. Best of 3 sets.

2

Serve

Underhand serve, ball must bounce once before hitting the wall.

3

Walls

Ball can hit walls and glass, adding strategic depth to the game.

4

Doubles Only

Always played in teams of two, perfect for social interaction.

Court Dimensions

  • • Court size: 20m x 10m
  • • Enclosed with walls and glass
  • • Net height: 88cm at center
  • • Service boxes: 3m x 3m
